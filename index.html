Know Before You Go

What makes or breaks it

🎟️ Arches timed-entry

Reserve your Day 4 entry window at recreation.gov as soon as it opens — summer slots go fast.

🐾 Dogs + desert heat

Day 4–5 hit 100°+. Never leave dogs in the car, watch hot pavement, and pack way more water than feels necessary.

🛏️ Book Springdale first

Zion's gateway town sells out earliest over July 4th weekend. It's your #1 reservation.

🚗 Friday-3rd traffic

Arriving the 3rd dodges the brutal Sunday return jam — but leave Vegas by midday to stay ahead of outbound holiday traffic.

⛽ Day 3 is the long one

Topeka → Glenwood is ~10 hr of wheel time. Fuel up, start early, and split driving if you can.
