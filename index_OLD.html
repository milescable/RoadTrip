Blast the flatlands. Savor the West.

Three big driving days carry you across the eastern interstates — then everything slows down for the Rocky Mountains, Utah's red rock, and a final run to the Pacific. Front-loaded miles, back-loaded magic.

🎆

You're driving over July 4th weekend. Book Moab (Jul 3) and Springdale (Jul 4) first — gateway towns sell out months ahead and prices spike. Grab Arches timed-entry for Jul 3 the second they're available. And on Sun Jul 5, clear Las Vegas before ~1 PM — I-15 back to LA is one of the worst traffic days of the year.

Know Before You Go

What makes or breaks it

🎆 It's July 4th weekend

Book Moab (Jul 3) & Springdale (Jul 4) ASAP — they sell out months ahead and prices surge over the holiday.

🚗 Beat the Jul 5 I-15 jam

Sunday return traffic from Vegas to LA is brutal. Clear Las Vegas before ~1 PM to avoid hours of standstill near Cajon Pass.

🎟️ Get the parks pass

The "America the Beautiful" pass ($80/yr) covers Colorado Nat'l Monument, Arches, Canyonlands & Zion — pays for itself easily.

📅 Reservations & shuttles

Arches timed-entry (Jul 3) + the Zion shuttle are the two logistics that trip people up. Sort both at recreation.gov.

💧 Respect the desert

Early-July days 5–7 hit 100–110°F. Carry far more water than feels necessary, hike at sunrise, never let the tank drop below half in the Mojave.
